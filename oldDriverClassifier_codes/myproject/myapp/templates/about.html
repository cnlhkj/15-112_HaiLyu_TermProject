Motivation

Sometimes you just want to do some simple classification over a small database, and you don't want to use those dumb softwares or write codes from scratch. Therefore, the maker wanted to make a website to make the process of analytics easier for users. And now, here it is.

About the Maker

The maker of this website is Hai Lyu. He is young and vibrant. He is also very handsome. But the most important thing is that he is very modest. That's why he did not show his face in the sliders.

He is also a member of Old Driver Club. That's why he wants to introduce a little bit about this mysterious organization.

About Old Driver Club

The Old Driver Club is a cult created by the one and only Yilong Change. The club is made up of people who believe in the power of technology. As cars are products of human beings' wisdom, Yilong used cars as the symbol of the club, and he proposed that the members called themselves "old drivers". Therefore, the club was named as "Old Driver Club".

During the process of making the website, the old drivers, especially Yilong himself, helped Hai Lyu a lot. Therefore, Hai decided to make this page to thank all the old drivers. If you want to learn more about this club, please feel free to contact Hai Lyu.

About Old Driver

Old Driver classifier is a small website designed for processing small dataset. It has three main funcionalities:

  • labelAuto-classification

    Find the best model among Naive Bayes, Decision Tree, Random Forest and Support Vector Machine for your dataset.

  • equalizerExploration

    Help you explore your dataset by proper visualization.

  • settings_ethernetDIY

    Tune parameters for the model by yourself.

Hope this website can be an old driver who could help you speed up the process of data analytics!
